About Jennie Kim

Kennie Kim (born 1996) is a South Korean singer and a member of the K-pop group Blackpink. She debuted in 2016 and released her solo hit “Solo” in 2018. In 2025, she released her first solo album, “Ruby.”

Important Events

  1. 2016 – Debuted as a member of BLACKPINK
  2. 2018 – Released her first solo single “SOLO.”
  3. 2023 – Acted in the HBO series “The Idol.”
  4. 2024 – Launched her own fashion brand, ODDATELIER

Key Contributions

  • Main rapper and vocalist of BLACKPINK
  • Global ambassador for Chanel, Calvin Klein
  • Founder of fashion label ODDATELIER
